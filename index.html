About Me


Hi! My name is Ginger Keys and I am currently a student at Base Camp Coding Academy in Water Valley, MS! Thank you so much for visiting my portfolio! Base Camp teaches me how to better myself as a person and as a Software Developer. Base Camp is really important to me and has influenced my life. You can learn more about some of my favorite things in the "Favorites" tab! I also enjoy reading and sometimes writing in my spare time. One of the biggest inspirations in my life is my father. He passed away in 2014 and he helped me be who I am currently. Although I am semi far away from some of my biggest supporters, Base Camp has truly changed my life.
